Mint Chicken

A favorite family recipe that my Mom has made for hundreds of guests. Fresh lemon juice and mint leaves are a must! Serve over hot cooked white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. Mix Chicken garlic, and toss with flour
  2. Heat margarine
  3. add chicken pieces cook/stir until browned
  4. Add Mint Leaves and lemon juice
  5. cover and steam for 5 minutes
